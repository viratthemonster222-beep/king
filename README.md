## Emotional Biases

Common emotional biases that affect investors:
- **Fear**: Causes panic selling and risk aversion
- **Greed**: Leads to overconfidence and excessive risk-taking
- **Overconfidence**: Results in concentrated portfolios
- **Herd Mentality**: Following crowd decisions blindly
- **Loss Aversion**: Disproportionate fear of losses vs. gains
- **Recency Bias**: Over-weighting recent events

## Best Practices

1. Create and stick to an investment plan
2. Automate investment decisions when possible
3. Diversify your portfolio
4. Focus on long-term goals
5. Review performance periodically (not constantly)
6. Seek professional advice when needed
